Adderall Home

ADDERALL HOME: THE PLACE TO GET HOOKED

Adderall home: We may not have had a choice. We either got on the drug or risked ourselves to never become a functioning part of society.

Our trouble was concentration; we just couldn’t do it. Our dysfunction was how to function socially. Our focus was nowhere near in focus.

Adderall was prescribed, and we had no say in the matter. Not that we would at such a young age in which we could take care of ourselves.

 

OUR PARENTS KNEW NO BETTER

Don’t blame them. Honestly, many people they knew no better. Being diagnosed for ADHD, then later being placed on Adderall, all Mom and Dad cared about was us getting better.

We were to be better focused, more aware, pointed, concentrated on what we needed to concentrated.

And the hoped-for result: Higher school marks, sharper intellect, stronger social skills, better friends – please let our kid have better friends, and not just acquaintances who choose to make fun of our kid’s ADHD.

And for a good while, the drug worked. It really did.

Everything stayed together without the slightest hint that all the way into our adulthood, we were becoming Adderall addicts.

 

ADDERALL HOME: THE PLACE NOT TO GET HEALED

As safe as innocuous as your child home might seem to you, it can be a bit difficult to think it was here, inside these strong walls and ceiling, that something still got inside under the auspices of helping to now hopelessly getting you addicted.

You see, Adderall is a stimulant. It has amphetamine properties to its chemical makeup. That’s what’s made you be able to focus all this time, to now, where you realize to continue your focus, you need to continue using the drug, which is called dependency.
Adderall home: the place not to get healed from your Adderall addiction. Adderall dependency and subsequent withdrawals can be very dangerous. At this point, your best option is to seek out a licensed rehabilitation facility staffed with licensed clinical workers. They can monitor your recovery, making sure you do so in the safest way possible, that can also guarantee your eventual sobriety.
